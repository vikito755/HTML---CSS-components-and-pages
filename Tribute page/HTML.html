Jack "Mad Jack" Churchil

The Sword-Wielding, Bagpipe-Playing Badass Of WWII.

"Any officer who goes into action without his sword is improperly dressed."
- Jack Churchil

He was one hell of a guy!


Here is a short list of his fortunes and misfortunes:


  1. Captured a post of around hundred men with only fifty

  2. Escaped a concentration camp twice.

  3. While traveling home with train he always threw his suitcase out of the window.

  4. Started in two movies

  5. Second place in the world archery competition in Oslo, Norway
